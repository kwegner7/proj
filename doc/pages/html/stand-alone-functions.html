Stand-Alone Functions to Compute Air Density and RHOR


Specify an altitude (feet) to obtain air density:

GetAirDensity -altitude 150000.0

The result is:

Air Density: 0.0011890 grams per centimeter cubed

Specify any 2 positions in 3-space (feet) to obtain the RHOR:

GetAirDepth -pos1 0.0, 10000.0, 100000.0 -pos2 20000.0, 10000.0, 150000.0

The result is:

Air Density at Position 1: 0.0011890 grams per centimeter cubed
Air Density at Position 2: 0.0011716 grams per centimeter cubed
         Air Depth (rhor): 35.9341 grams per centimeter squared
